    The Science Behind
    MENO Vaginal Moisture

    The Biology

    Estrogen is the key driver for maintaining vaginal tissue structure. As estrogen levels decline when we get older, vaginal walls begin to thin, which can cause dryness and discomfort.

    How This Helps

    MENO Vaginal Moisture contains clinically-studied amounts of both pueraria mirifica and fenugreek extracts, both shown to have estrogenic effects. This means they can mimic estrogen to help support healthy vaginal tissue & moisture.*

    The Biology

    For comfortable, enjoyable sex, increasing blood flow to the vagina is crucial, because blood delivers naturally-lubricating fluid to the vagina. As we get older, blood vessel dilation and circulation becomes less efficient, which negatively impacts vaginal lubrication.

    How This Helps

    MENO Vaginal Moisture is formulated with a clinically-studied amount of ginkgo leaf extract, a nootropic known to produce nitric oxide. Nitric oxide helps dilate (open) blood vessels to support healthy blood flow and circulation, which can result in more comfortable sex.*

    The Biology

    Healthy feelings of intimacy and getting “in the mood” require comfort & relaxation. As we get older, decreased estrogen levels and fluctuating neurotransmitters can cause symptoms like mood swings, brain fog, and lacking feelings of passion.

    How This Helps

    This formula includes maca, an herb traditionally used for its mood-balancing effects. It also contains a clinically-studied dose of saffron extract, which is shown to support healthy mood and feelings of intimacy.*
